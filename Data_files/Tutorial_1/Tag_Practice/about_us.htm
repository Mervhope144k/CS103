Organizational Genius & Company             Creates Space.  Saves Time.  Frees  Your Mind.                                                                                             


        We are Sarah Paluch and Heather Lorentz, co-owners of Organizational Genius and Company, a new company available to the Western New York population, dedicated to getting you organized.  We started out by organizing our parents' homes for the enjoyment of it.  We shared tips and tricks.  We traded book shelves for cabinets, labelers for laminators.  She shopped for all the organizational tools imaginable. As we went from one project on to the next we explored the limits of organizing. We organized basements, bedrooms, drawers, closets, tools, and garages.  At this time, a friend surreptitiously mentioned to a local radio station KISS 98.5 that we were the organizers that they needed to get their disc jockeys' office organized. So we organized their office and chronicled our progress simultaneously on live radio!

We are flexible to various modes in which to assist our clients.   That is, we can do the organizing for you or we can coach you through the process so that you get the practice in doing it while we assist. In either case, we will always follow up to help you plan how you will maintain your system.  And we review the process with you.  We are also flexible to the job that you request, be it to organize your "junk" drawer, photo album, basement or entire house.

We are committed to our lives' priorities and we believe that an organized home/office encourages productivity in the work we do so that we can then attend to these priorities.  In this respect, organization is empowering.  We believe also:

  •  All of us can be organized and stay organized.
  •  The right materials and tools improve organization.
  • Organizing and designing a space complement each other.

We plan to expand our services and this web site to include an online support newsgroup (forum) for active organizers to share their ideas and triumphs.  We also will add more resources such as links to other similar web sites and to stores with organizational tools.  We also plan to offer to speak for groups and offer  workshops.  We want to expand our e-store product offerings and to provide professional organizational services to entire state of New York.
